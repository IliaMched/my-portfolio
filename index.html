About Me

I am the founder of a growing clothing brand that has been gaining traction. My current goal is to develop a fully functional, custom-coded mobile and desktop website for the brand. Currently, I utilize Squarespace with additional custom code, but I aspire to transition to a platform built entirely from scratch.

Previously, I interned as a front-end web developer at a digital marketing agency, where I honed my skills in HTML, JavaScript, and responsive design. During this role, I created optimized HTML email templates and landing pages tailored for seamless performance across all major devices. My work included developing email campaigns for prominent clients such as CIBC, BMW, Johnson & Johnson, and others. While it's been some time since I last coded extensively, I am eager to refresh and refine my technical skills to achieve my goals.
